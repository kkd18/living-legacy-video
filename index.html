Why would I want to share my story?

You may be thinking "why would I want to share my story?" and that is an excellent question! Deciding what you leave behind in the world after you are gone is a big decision! But that is why we are here to help. Recording your stories and memories allows your family to remember you for years to come. No one wants to leave their loved ones behind hoping that they will remember how much you love them, so why not just tell them yourself! By leaving a legacy behind, you are allowing your family the option to never forget your voice and for some, this is everything. Speaking from experience, I would love to still be able to listen to my mothers many stories and to hear her say I love you again.

I also wish I could gift my daughters with the many memories of their grandmother. That is the whole reason I created Living Legacy Video. I want you to be able to give your family a gift for generations to come. This may seem like a big decision. Our mission is to genuinely help you leave your loved ones with your story. Everyone's story is unique. With the help of us, you can choose from services such as one story for all, multiple dvd's, and adding pictures and music. We'll walk with you step by step to make the process easy and the end result a masterpiece. So why wouldn't you want to leave your legacy? There really isn't a good reason which is why we are the perfect company to aid

you in this process. If you are here, you have a choice as to how you want to be remembered. Your video story can be a gift of love to share for generations to come. Whether you believe it or not, someone you love will cherish your story after you're gone. All I have left are pictures and memories that have faded over time of the family I long to hear and see again. But you can ensure that none of your family will feel this way. It is important for us to preserve our life story for those we love and have not met yet.
