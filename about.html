Our Story:

I’m Denise O’Leary and I want to help you preserve your special story.I created Living Legacy after the loss of several loved ones whos memories began to fade with time. Living Legacy provides you the opportunity to leave your memories for generations to come. As wonderful as they are, photos, portraits, and even home videos are an incomplete legacy. How wonderful would it be to capture a fuller picture of your life, your interests, and your uniqueness? So why not create “a living legacy” about you? I would give anything to hear my mom’s voice, see my mom’s smile, and feel her presence again. It’s amazing what we can convey through a video. My girls would also have a glimpse of their grandma.

Through our simple three step process, we can help you turn your life story into a time capsule so that your loved ones will never have to carry the burden of no longer hearing your voice. We walk you through every step of the way to ensure you end up with a masterpiece. Our lives are not ones that should be forgotten because every moment we spend on earth is a gift. Our children and grandchildren deserve to know where they come from who we were during our time on earth. Therefore, Our mission is to enable you to leave your loved ones with a story about you... by you... for future generations. If you want to leave your legacy, than let me help you record memories so that your legacy will live on.
